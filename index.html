A little about me!

Hi there! My name is Alisa Mai Tran, and I am a second-year Global Business & Digital Arts student at the University of Waterloo. I'm an optimistic and dedicated individual seeking opportunities to gain experience in the UX/UI design field. I am passionate about all things creative.

Why Seals?

My favourite animal of all time are SEALS, specifically Baikal seals! I just think they’re so adorable with their big eyes and floppy little flippers! They’re such playful and expressive creatures that can frolic on land and water. One of my bucket list items is to see a seal in person one day!

I try to encapsulate the free-flowing, fluid nature of seals through my creative work. Whether it’s through soft and organic shapes to lively, experimental designs, I approach design with an open mind, adapting and having fun as the process unfolds.
