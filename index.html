A little about me!

Hi there! My name is Alisa Mai Tran, and I am a second-year Global Business & Digital Arts student at the University of Waterloo, hoping to pursue a minor in French Studies. Beyond graphic design, I love expanding my horizons into related and non-related fields, such as cinematic film and production, 3D modelling, fashion, poetry and literature.

Why Seals?

My favourite animal of all time are SEALS, specifically Baikal seals! I just think they’re so adorable with their big eyes and floppy little flippers! They’re such playful and expressive creatures that can frolic on land and water. In fact, one of my bucket list items is to see a seal in person one day!

During the design process, I try to encapsulate the free-flowing, fluid nature of seals through my creative work. Whether it’s through soft and organic shapes to lively, experimental designs, I approach design with an open mind, adapting and having fun as the process unfolds.
